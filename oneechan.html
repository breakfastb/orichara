saki

  • height: 162cm
  • age: 22
  • (semi) recent college grad

An outwardly playful, open girl, Saki is almost completely inept with most common household tasks. Cleaning, cooking, dishes...she somehow manages to muck it up at least half of the time, despite her best efforts. An English major graduate, she is currently in limbo post-grad and unsure of the future, after something happened with a previous girlfriend that left her debilitated for some time. She is currently living at Sky Apartments by the grace of her landlord (her friend from uni). She likes beer, video games, and pizza, but abhors work, crowded trains/buses, and veggies.

ume

  • height: 162cm
  • age: 19
  • 2nd year in college

Being the oldest of two children in a single mother household, Ume is well-acquainted with the housework of daily life and excels in studies. As a hopeful to-be law student, she is used to spending a lot of her time either studying or taking care of her little sister. When she came to Sky Apartments to dorm, she didn't expect to end up gaining a new little-big sister - Saki. A capable but somewhat awkward girl.

the two of them

Ume is the sort who can't leave others alone (even if she wants to), and Saki is the sort who depends on others. After meeting the night after Ume moved in (incidentally, because Saki got locked out of her apartment again), they came to know each other and grew closer.

Although Saki is the older one, she often relies much on Ume for emotional support among other things. Although Ume complains, she finds it somewhat comforting and truly loves her.
