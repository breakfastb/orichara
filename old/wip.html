artist+engineer

Artist is a freelancer who ran away from home to pursue her dreams once she left high school. Now a young adult, she lives with a few other artist friends. By a stroke of luck, she gets her work displayed at the local art museum, and it is at the gallery showing that she meets Engineer.

Engineer is the daughter of a single father and is a bit standoffish (perhaps taking after him in that respect). She has a natural love for math and enjoys looking at beautiful things. Her first year at uni, she is walking home to her apartment after class and passes the gallery. Intrigued, she walks in to check it out, and meets Artist.

delinquent+teacher

Delinquent comes from a broken home, with parents who are never around and older brothers who grew up just as rough as she did. Despite her rough speech and manner, however, she isn't bad, and she enjoys things like writing and sports.

Teacher is a teacher who's been working at Delinquent's school for a year. Just starting to get the hang of things, her school life is turned upside down when she meets Delinquent in unusual circumstances.
