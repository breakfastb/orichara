tomboy+megane

Two characters I created on a whim and continue to draw on a whim. While I normally draw them in NSFW scenarios, this is more just a nature of the concept that spawned them versus an actual innate lewdness about them. The glasses girl is the top in their relationship and tends to take the lead instead of the the tomboy, despite the tomboy's image as a cool and experienced lover. The tomboy finds herself weak at the knees with her glasses-clad lover, and is a bit shy about it.
