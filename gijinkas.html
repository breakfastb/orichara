Gijinkas of various music streaming services, including Pandora, Youtube Music, Spotify, and Soundcloud.

Pandora: A bit insecure and sensitive, but branching out and becoming a little bolder. One of the more popular of the group, although she doesn't really acknowledge it. Usually great at giving recommendations, but has more mainstream taste.

YT Music: The youngest of the group. Despite that, she is one of the boldest! Doesn't like to be left alone, and will pout if you try to leave the house without her (if you're not subscribed). More varied taste in music.

Spotify: Generally a bit mysterious but refined, Spotify is a bit of an elitist. She likes creating mixes.

Soundcloud: Although she isn't the most popular, she doesn't let that stop her! Generally gears towards more indie music, and thus has some of the most obscure taste across the bunch. Although she has a bit of a shy streak, she loves being able to share music. Gets along with YT Music.
